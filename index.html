Clinical Foundations
01
Cognitive Behavioural Hypnotherapy
CBH is the backbone of everything I do. It combines clinical hypnotherapy with CBT and mindfulness — so we're working with your conscious mind and your subconscious at the same time. That's what makes it stick. You don't just understand your patterns intellectually — you rewire them at the level where they actually live.
02
Rapid Transformational Therapy
RTT gets to the root. Instead of managing symptoms for months, we go straight to the origin — the moment a belief was formed, the decision your younger self made to survive. Once you see it clearly, it loses its grip. This isn't slow. It's precise.

The backbone is cognitive behavioural hypnotherapy (CBH) — an evidence-based clinical framework that integrates CBT (structured cognitive and behavioural tools) with clinical hypnosis. Research shows that adding hypnosis to CBT produces significantly greater improvement than CBT alone. CBH provides the structure: identifying the beliefs driving your patterns, disputing them with precision, and then using hypnosis to install the new beliefs at the subconscious level — bridging the gap between what you know and what you actually feel.

Alongside CBH, I draw on RTT (Rapid Transformational Therapy®) when regression to root-cause moments is the right tool, and somatic release work for what the body is holding that the mind can't reach. These three modalities integrate into The Inner Shift Method.

As for hypnosis itself: it's a state of focused, relaxed attention — not unconsciousness, not a stage trick. You're fully aware and in complete control throughout. Most people describe it as the most focused they've ever felt.
