What these services are

I offer cognitive behavioural hypnotherapy, rapid transformational therapy (RTT), and somatic coaching sessions. These are complementary therapeutic services — they are not psychotherapy, psychiatry, or medical treatment.

My work supports personal development, behavioural change, and emotional well-being. It does not replace medical advice, psychiatric care, or licensed psychotherapy. If you are in crisis or experiencing a psychiatric emergency, please contact your local emergency services or a licensed mental health professional.

Who this is for

My services are available to adults (18+) who are seeking support with patterns, habits, anxiety, confidence, self-worth, and related areas. Sessions are conducted online via video call.

During the intake process, I assess whether we're a good fit. I reserve the right to decline or discontinue working with someone if I believe another type of professional support would be more appropriate.

Professional boundaries

I am a certified RTT Practitioner and a CBH Hypnotherapist in training (completing the Level 5 Diploma at the UK College of Hypnosis & Hypnotherapy). I operate within the scope of my training and professional ethics.

I do not diagnose medical or psychiatric conditions. I do not prescribe medication. I do not provide emergency or crisis intervention services.

Confidentiality

Everything shared in sessions is confidential. I will not disclose information about you to third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law or where there is a serious and imminent risk of harm to you or others.
